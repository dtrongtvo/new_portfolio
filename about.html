Hey! Nice to meet you ^^

Hi, I'm Trong, a young product designer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. For me, designing is not just about creating a beautiful mockup, but also about solving problems to improve people's lives (like creating an app to help people decide where to have meals with friends at the weekend, for example :>)

I used to pursue front-end development and spent a year learning it. Later on, I realized that what I truly love is UX/UI design since I enjoy creating beautiful designs and exploring human psychology more than spending time fixing tons of bugs ><. The more I learn, the more fascinated I become by the meanings behind the creation and design of products around me. I always hope that one day, I can create a product (even a simple one) that helps solve big problems, especially to help people understand themselves more deeply (as understanding ourselves is not easy, is it? :D)

When putting design aside, I usually spend time reading books, listening to podcasts, or learning to draw some pieces of pixel art as my hobbies ^^
